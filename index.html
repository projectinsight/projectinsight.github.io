Project Insight

What Purpose Will We Serve?

We are building a modern and customizable take on standard task tracking and other project management related details. We feel the current offerings, as amazing as they are, are just missing that "one" feature that would put them over the top and we plan to deliver not just a project management tool that lets you track tasks, but one that efficiently and easily track time spent (which is a resource of it's own) and better plan the future of your project.

We plan to bring you the insight you expect when managing your projects!

How Will It Look?

We plan to leverage the concepts set forth with the Material Design document to bring a clean, visually appealing and modern user experience that will be built with the idea in mind of empowering not just the project managers but the people who will be consuming the tasks as well. We aspire to make the experience pleasant for all users, regardless of role. To help us achieve this visual look we will be using the Material Design Lite JavaScript framework.

Aside from just the visuals, we plan to leverage great technologies from other companies to make the site responsive to interactions and update without the need for user iteraction allowing your team to remain in sync with one another easily.
